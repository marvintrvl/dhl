Introducing Our Sustainable Packaging Innovation

Eco-friendly, Secure, and Protective Packaging Solutions

Introducing CARL

CARL stands for Climate Awareness, Accessibility, Reusable Packaging, and Logistics. Inspired by Carl von Carlowitz, our innovative packaging solution focuses on sustainability and efficiency.

Why Choose Our Packaging?

Eco-friendly

Made from recycled plastic, our packaging reduces waste and promotes sustainability. Just like Tupperware, it minimizes waste compared to disposable containers.

Secure

Equipped with GPS tracking, our packaging ensures you can monitor your package's location, preventing lost parcels. It's like having a tiny tracker on your Tupperware, ensuring it doesn't get lost in the fridge (or on the delivery truck!).

Protective

The weather-resistant, two-layer package with a honeycomb structure in between safeguards your items from damage. It's similar to how Tupperware keeps your leftovers safe from getting crushed.

How It Works

Sustainable Production

Our packaging is produced sustainably, utilizing recycled plastic. To cover the costs of GPS trackers and production, we print advertisements from companies on the boxes and envelopes.

Paper-Free Delivery

We use a permanently printed label that dynamically links to the corresponding database entry, ensuring a paper-free delivery process without changing printing labels.

Deposit System

To encourage the return of parcels and envelopes, customers pay a deposit upon placing the order, which is credited to their DHL/Deutsche Post account when the packaging is returned to a DHL station or driver.
